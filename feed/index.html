Abhi & Priya

Aditya was a producer at Vogue when he met Lisa for a show and they hit it off instantly. Amid the fun and banter of the arc-lights, the senior Ms. Haydon casually mentioned that he would be a good fit for her sister Julia! Little did she know she had christened herself Cupid as she bumped into him a week later with little sis in tow.

 

As she excitedly introduced them, abrupt hellos were exchanged and that’s that! An awkward silence ensued… So much so for her prediction that the two would get on like a house on fire. As they parted, she forced them to exchange numbers and that started the ball rolling. Aditya texted Julia to apologise for the awkward encounter and asked if he could take her out to dinner to make up for the tragedy. She said yes. 

 

As Julia was getting ready for their first date, he called requesting her to pick him up. Amused at this ‘different’ guy, she was even more curious to meet him now. They met and he explained why he had to ask her to pick him up and they laughed about it. As they had dinner and drinks on a rooftop terrace, the evening slipped into effortlessly fun conversation and “one of the best dates” they had ever had. It felt like the beginning of something special.

 

 A few dates later, they knew they had found a love that made them feel alive – a love that felt electric!

NATASA & HARDIK

As a young ballerina from Serbia, she was supposed to take a flight to Istanbul but landed up in Mumbai in a last minute change of plan. From there on, their lives took trajectories they never imagined they’d traverse. The starkly opposite and determined people bumped into each other at the unlikeliest of places one evening when this polished young model noticed a funky, carefree man wearing a hat & chains at 1 am. They got talking, and soon found themselves lost in conversation. Along the journey that ensued, each was on the other’s team through the highs and lows, which was all that mattered to them. This unspoken bond blossomed and he proposed to his lady love on a cruise in Dubai, a month before Covid hit, derailing their wedding plans.

 

Three years later, they decided to pick up from where they left, and celebrated their wedding by renewing their vows in the midst of their families and friends, and most importantly, their tiny little one – Agastya.

 

Honouring their respective cultures, they got married in a Hindu ceremony, as well as a Christian wedding, and danced their hearts out at their Sangeet – needless to say, both these terrific dancers clearly nailed it!

PANKHURI & KRUNAL

It was a matter of pure luck when Pankhuri bumped into cricketer Hardik Pandya during one of her event management stints and he decided to pick up the phone to introduce her to his brother Krunal for some work. Krunal met Pankhuri soon after and they both got along like a house on fire in their very first meeting.

 

Months of friendship soon paved way for romantic dates and before they knew, they began to belong to each other in an honest way. They had fostered a companionship where they could discuss just about anything in the world without the fear of being judged. In him, she had found a friend, lover and soul mate and together they were ready to walk hand-in-hand through each others’ battles and insecurities in life. 

 

He proposed to his lady love on the night he clinched the title of “Man of the match” at the finals of IPL 2017 as it was the most important day of his life. She almost missed the proposal while trying to shield her face from the cake that she thought would land on her face!  She finally said yes and as they charmed their way into each other’s hearts, they made the world around a little brighter, a little rosier and a lot more fun with their infectious enthusiasm and laughter.  

 

When Pankhuri contacted us to film her wedding, we met up with a beautiful, soft spoken person with innocent, warm and smiling eyes. Ten minutes into the conversation we knew we had to film this wedding and our meeting with Krunal cemented that gut feeling. In the process, we’ve made friends with two of the most genuine people we’ve come across. The festivities took place in Mumbai at a three day energetic extravaganza of fun, friends & laughter.
